## 🌍 Problem Statement  

**Personalized Career & Skills Advisor**  
A platform designed to guide learners and professionals in making **informed career choices** by analyzing their skills, interests, and aspirations.  

---

## 🎯 Goal  

To provide an **AI-driven advisor system** that:  
- Identifies strengths and weaknesses  
- Suggests suitable career paths  
- Recommends skills to acquire  
- Assists in long-term career growth  

---

## ✨ Features Offered  

- 🔍 **Skill Assessment** – Identify current capabilities  
- 📊 **Gap Analysis** – Highlight areas for improvement  
- 🎯 **Career Recommendations** – Suggest optimal paths based on interests and skillsets  
- 📚 **Learning Resources** – Curated materials for growth  
- 📈 **Progress Tracking** – Visual dashboard for continuous development
